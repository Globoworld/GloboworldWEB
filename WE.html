Our Journey

The website Globoworld, hosted at https://globosearch.rf.gd/, was initially born as a search engine, as indicated by its name, Globosearch. However, the vision expanded beyond just a search engine.

It was decided to create many other tools, evolving the site into Globotools. Yet, the name did not inspire enough, leading to a rebranding as Globoservices. Unfortunately, even this name lacked the desired impact, prompting another change to Globoworld.

After some time as Globoworld, we decided to update the site with a new slogan: "Born to make your life better." This reflects our mission to enhance your daily experience.

The website has been in operation since August 11, 2024, and continues to serve users to this day. Starting from July 5, 2025 Due to maintenance issues the webiste will be hosted at https://globoworld.github.io/GloboworldWEB/ but you can still use the old domain, you will get automatically redirected to our new domain.
